FOOTBALL ISLAND

Teaching Guide

  1. Show video
  2. Using a quick write or pair-share format, engage students in reflection using the following prompts:
    • What does it mean to persevere?
    • What was the team's goal, and what obstacles got in their way?
    • How did the team peresevere?
    • What is a goal of yours?
    • What are some obstacles that get in your way of accomplishing this goal?
    • What can you do to persevere?
  3. Close by asking students to recognize in writing a person they have witnessed persevere.

Objective:

Students will compare and contrast obstacles to success in their lives to the obstacles faced by the players of the football team.
